The Atelier

A room the size of
one conversation.

26 Beauté is not a chain. It is a single, soft-lit room at 58-1 Jalan SL 1/3, where the same hands have cleared the same neighbourhood's skin for over ten years.

We do one face at a time. We close the door. We talk only as much as the treatment needs. Our regulars come because nothing — not a package, not a product, not a subscription — is ever pushed across the counter.

— with care, the 26 team

Our Philosophy

We sell
nothing but
the facial.

I. No hard sell, ever.

If you leave 26 Beauté, you leave with calmer skin and no bag of products you didn't ask for. Packages are optional and always explained in plain Ringgit.

II. Price is the price.

Our menu is the menu. Students, regulars, first-timers — all pay the same honest rate for the same honest work.

III. Slow hands, steady skin.

Good skin isn't urgent. Most of our guests come monthly, for years. We'd rather see you five times than sell you once.

IV. A small, warm room.

No loud lobbies. No rotating staff. You will likely be tended to by the same beautician every visit.
